Problem

These days, playing a quality video game (that has more than an hour of content and isn't filled with micro-transactions) costs a suprising amount. At least 200$ for a console, and then at least 30$ for each game (though indie games do give some relief in that regard). This means less people can have fun and be inspired by video games.

It is also astronmically hard to make a game of your own and put it on a console. We wish to allow people to exercise their creativity by creating experiences that they and others will enjoy. This leads to people getting demotiviated and causing a lack of creativity and ideas in gaming.
